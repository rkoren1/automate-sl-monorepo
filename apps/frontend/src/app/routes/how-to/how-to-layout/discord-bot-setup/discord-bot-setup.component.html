How to setup Discord bot

Discord Chat Relay allows you to link second life group with you discord channel, meaning you will be able to see messages and talk to people in second life group without loging into the game.
  1. Go to the bot settings and click on discord setttings
  2. You need to fill the whole form for bot to work properly:
    3. Webhook URL:
    Click on the gear icon next to the channel name (it only appears once you hover over the channel name with the mouse).

    Select Integrations on the left. Create a new webhook or use existing one.
    SL Group UUID: Also known as Group Key, it can be found in the group profile.
    Discord Channel ID: To see Discord Channel ID, Discord developer mode needs to be activated (How to enable developer mode on Discord). After it is activated simply right click on the channel name and select Copy Channel ID.
  3. And now click on the discord icon to add the AutomateSL Discord bot to your discord server.
